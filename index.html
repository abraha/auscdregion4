        Welcome

        African Union Sport Council Region 4

        The African Union Sports Council Region 4 is a specialized Technical Office of the African Union Sport Council responsible for sports. AUSC was established through the endorsement of the Statute of AUSC by the Assembly of African Heads of States and Governments in 2016. Its mandate is co-ordination of the African Sports Movement and being a forum for concerted action between Member States for the promotion and development of sports and development through sport in Africa.
